Float Hybrid Interactive
Anything Interactive

Float Hybrid Entertainment is a San Francisco based interactive agency with roots in movies and games. Founded by John Gaeta, Float's initial focus was natural gesture-based interfaces. Which later evolved to general mastery on combining design with emerging technologies.

During my time in Float I have worked on variety of interfaces and displays from tiny screens of Google Glass to 65" 4K monitors, from WiP AR headsets to Oculus Developer Kit. I created interfaces that use Kinect to see you as well as interfaces that you use various headsets to see it. I worked on single page web apps, IoT, games, mobile applications and websites. My main technologies were Unity, HTML/CSS/JS and node.js

The projects included prototypes for trade shows like CES, protypes for internal labs, products relased on appstores, installations for events like Football World Cup, SXSW and Anything Interactive, an in-house interactive framework.

For latest information please see the Float website.

Installations

Anything Interactive was used in multiple installations including multiple stores in San Francisco International Airport, Jumpman Toronto, Wingtip San Francisco, multiple tradeshows and internal client labs.
