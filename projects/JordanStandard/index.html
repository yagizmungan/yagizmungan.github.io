The Jordan Standard
Jordan Standard Trailer

The Jordan Standard is an interactive drill experience where the user perform a number of basketball drills and get compared to Jordan.

Driven by multiple kinects the experience features a variety of games and drills involving offence, defense, dribbling and posture.

Installations

The Jordan Standard first aired at 2016 all star game in Toronto as an event specific installation and currently it is deployed in Toronto as a permanent attraction.
