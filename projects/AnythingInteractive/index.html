Anything Interactive

Anything Interactive is a sensor-based platform that Float has created. It allowed us to turng any object/surface into natural-gesture driven interfaces. It also leveraged multiple IoT platforms to expand.

We have used sensors like Kinect, Kinect v2, Intel RealSense as well as of-the-shelf motion sensors to turn analog surfaces and objects into natural and smart interactive experiences. The system came with tools to manage content, interactivity, troubleshoot issues and collect/display data.

For latest information please see the Anything Interactive website.

Installations

Anything Interactive was used in multiple installations including multiple stores in San Francisco International Airport, Jumpman Toronto, Wingtip San Francisco, multiple tradeshows and internal client labs.
