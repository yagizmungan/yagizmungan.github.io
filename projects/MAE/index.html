MÆ - Motion Afterefffect is the result of the artist's residency where Freya and I collaborated to question the relation between virtual and physical bodies, gender roles in emerging experiences among. Starting with Freya's existing research we used residency to turn some of our ideas/questions into performance.

The performance featured multiple scenes that relied on Freya's brilliant performance, found footage and interactive technology.
