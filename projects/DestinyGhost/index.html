Destiny 2 Ghost Skill

A companion Alexa skill for Destiny 2

An Alexa skill that can be used to get real time assitance while enjoying the game. The skill allowed you to change your loadout, learn about limited time events. Find and ping your allies.

AKQA designed and developed the Alexa skill, while a 3rd party created an optional hardware for the Ghost. My personal involvement was prototyping the architecture with a simple end-to-end experience and figuring out the needs and challenges for the production of the software.

The skill won Silver Lion in Cannes 2018
