Watson Primer

Watson Primer is an interactive exprience that can teach you about IBM Watson: why, how, how to get started.

Created with a great team at AKQA. The Watson Primer is composed of an interactive video wall, interactive floor, multiple kiosk and interactive projections. All the systems are connected to each other and overal creates an immersive room.

So far the Watson Primer is presented at Dreamforce 2017, CES 2018 and IBM Think 2018.
