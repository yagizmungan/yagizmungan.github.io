À Quatre Mains
protoype/work-in-progress

This page is at the moment a living proposal for À Quatre Mains. The approximately-current version can be accessed with the link above.

Prototype status:

  • Very very rough visuals but imagine in a human-sized version of this in VR. (Actually this album cover was something I discovered couple months into the project...). Each hexagon is within arm's reach and is a synthesizer.
  • Not tested in VR yet, even though everything is set for WebVR
  • WiP audio
  • Currently works with mouse (eventually will Oculus Touch/Vive hand controls):
    • Hover over each hexagon to trigger a synth
    • While hovering over a hexagon left click to change mode of the synthesizer
    • Drag and move mouse for rotating around
  • Multi-performer mode WiP
  • Obviously will go through many major/minor tweaks and polishes
